Getting Started

Welcome to our support page! Here you will find resources to help you navigate and use the system effectively.

  • Managing Products: Add, update, or delete products from your inventory.
  • Managing Customers: Add new customers, update their details, or view their information.
  • Billing: Create new bills, view today's bills, and manage billing history.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I add a new product? Navigate to "Manage Products" and click "Add Product" to input new product details.
  • How can I update customer information? Go to "Manage Customers," select the customer, and choose "Edit" to update details.
  • What should I do if I forget my password? Use the "Change Password" option in your profile menu to reset your password.
